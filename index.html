My Story

Many of us have experienced traumatic events even at the earliest of ages. I carried the pain from my childhood in silence, smiling through the pain. I felt deeply insecure and inadequate, learning very early on how to give my power and essence away in relationships. Because I was terrified of being alone, I allowed the boundaries I set to be repeatedly and callously ignored. I became disconnected from my own feelings and needs, abandoning myself and my dreams somewhere along the way.

I was an unwed mother at the age of 23 and had two failed marriages by the time I was 47. A marriage should be the safest place to share your deepest hurts, your biggest sins, the ugliest things about yourself as well as the most beautiful. A place where you’re not frightened of the one you give your heart to. After my second divorce, I walked away with the understanding that when safety is missing, there is no relationship. Finally, I had had enough. I was on a mission to find out why I kept choosing the same type of men, when I came across Conscious Uncoupling. What I discovered was more empowering than I could have imagined. Working through the steps of the program, I became aware of and also willing to take responsibility for my part in the harmful cycles I was playing out with others. When you’re able to lovingly accept your part in co-creating the good and bad relationships in your life, you then become empowered to create the life and relationships you’re worthy of.

I am now in a loving, supportive, and mutually respectful relationship where we share open communication. I’m a certified Conscious Uncoupling Coach and have my life coaching certification through the Jay Shetty Certification School in London, which is accredited by the Association for Coaching®. There is no easy way to have your heart broken; however, if you want to free yourself from the past, and the limiting beliefs and behaviors keeping you stuck while at the same time learn how to safely be in a relationship where you speak your truth, I encourage you to schedule a free 30-minute discovery call with me. I’m looking forward to guiding you to grow beyond your pain and struggle.
