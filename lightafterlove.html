The Fragile Foundation: Why Trust is Everything in a Relationship

What do you do when the foundation of your relationship cracks beneath your feet? Trust doesn’t explode—it splinters. Quietly, painfully, and often without warning. Dating after divorce isn’t about rushing back into love—it’s about healing, rediscovering yourself, and moving forward with intention.
